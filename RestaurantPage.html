Explore Aalsmeer

Discover your Gateway to Dutch Beauty and Adventure!!

Hungry?

Aalsmeer blends Dutch and international cuisine. Restaurants offer traditional Dutch dishes like stamppot and international flavors like Italian and Indonesian. Enjoy waterside dining with picturesque views of Westeinderplassen. Aalsmeer's diverse eateries cater to all tastes, making dining here a memorable experience.

The Rock

The Rock restaurant offers innovative fusion cuisine with stunning lakeside views. It's a must-visit for a unique dining experience by Westeinderplassen Lake. Weekly opening hours: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM.

The Pantry

The Pantry Restaurant is a charming culinary haven. It specializes in traditional Dutch cuisine, serving classic dishes like stamppot and Dutch pancakes, known as pannenkoeken. Nestled in the heart of Aalsmeer, it offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere, providing a delightful taste of Dutch culinary heritage. Weekly opening hours:1:00 PM - 9:00 PM.

Himalaya Palace Restaurant

Himalaya Palace Restaurant is a gastronomic treasure. This eatery specializes in authentic Indian and Nepali cuisine, offering a rich tapestry of flavors and aromas. Nestled in the heart of Aalsmeer, it provides a warm and inviting atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for a delightful culinary journey. Weekly opening hours: 12:00 PM - 10:00 PM.
